FAQ

What is a Hackathon?
A real time science fair! Program and build a project with a group of people to compete for prizes and industry recognition.
Where the HACK is The Bit Factory?
Suite 511, 526 S Main St, Akron, OH 44311
What should I bring?
Student ID, clothes, toiletries, hacker setup, an idea, and anything else you need to function independently for 24 hours.
Who can participate?
Any currently enrolled student, or who has graduated within 12 months.
How much does it cost?
Nothing! Zip! Nada! Attending is absolutely FREE!
Do I need to come with a team?
Nope! If you do not have a team we will help you find one. (Max of 4 people per team)
